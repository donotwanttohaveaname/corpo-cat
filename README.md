# corpo cat

a pixel game about zheng, a gay chinese boy with a SaaS job and an abyssinian cat called kinu.

kinu vomits every morning (that's the alarm clock). his boss sandal is a swedish HR nightmare. his girlfriends anna, susan and joy are going to malaga in 5 days. he has to earn €3000 or watch the stories from his couch.

32 branching endings for the situationship story arc. 115+ distractions. one spank button.

play: https://donotwanttohaveaname.github.io/corpo-cat/

## local
open `index.html` in any browser.
